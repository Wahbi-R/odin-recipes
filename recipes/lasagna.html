Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a fine food. It tends to be made in a layered form with a pasta sheet, meat, sauce, cheese, repeating over itself multiple times.

It's great and easy to make for groups of people!

Ingredients

    1 medium yellow onion 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 pound lean ground beef 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper 1 (24 to 25-ounce) jar marinara sauce(3 cups) 12 ounces low-moisture mozzarella cheese, shredded (about 3 cups), divided 15 dry lasagna noodles 15 to 16 ounces whole-milk ricotta cheese (about 2 cups)
