Beef Tacos

Description

Its just beef tacos man

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Taco shell
  • Salsa
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the beef however you want
  2. Cook your taco shells as instructed on the box
  3. Add your beef to the taco shells
  4. Add salsa, then the lettuce then top with the cheese
  5. Enjoy it while its hot
